Boys Are Good!

Boys Are Good!

Boys are good! They are all too often simply misunderstood. There have been many factors involved in this misunderstanding including the media glorifying girls and doing the opposite for boys. This site can help you get some understanding of boys and their unique and wonderful qualities.

This page will offer some resources to help bring understanding to boys and their unique ways. There has been a great deal of research that has not been published in the mainstream that is instrumental in our understanding. The books in the sidebar are one path to gain understanding and the blog posts on this page will give some initial ideas about boys and their wonderful differences.

The page is hosted by Tom Golden, LCSW who has spent many years working with boys and men who experienced loss and trauma. This page offers a bit of what he learned over those years. More about Tom
